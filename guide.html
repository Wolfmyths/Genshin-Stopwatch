Stamina

The stamina category has two inputs: Current Stamina & Desired stamina

  • Current stamina is how much resin you currently have.
  • Desired stamina is how much you want to have, which is set to 160 by default.

Parametric Transformer

The parametric transformer is a gadget that hands you multiple types of character/weapon enhancement materials for a large amount of enemy drops.

It takes 7 days to recover from it's cooldown once used, therefore there's only one duration to choose from.

Respawns

The respawn category covers everything in the game that respawns once defeated or looted such as:

  • Wildlife (12 Hours)
  • Enemies (12 Hours)
  • Local Specialties (48 hours)
  • Flaming and Mist Flowers (48 hours)
  • Ores (48-72 Hours depending on which ore)

For more information see the genshin impact fandom wiki

Expedition

Expeditions are passive tasks to send your characters on, the longer the expedition the greater the rewards.

To access expeditions, go to any adventurer's guild booth and talk to Katheryne.

Teapot Gardening/Construction

In the Serenita Teapot you can farm plants and create structures, plants/props will take 12-70 hours to grow/construct.

Realm Currency

Depending on the player's rank and status of their teapot, the player can earn a currency overtime. This currency is used to buy various items and materials at Tubby's shop and is worth the effort.

This timer requires 2 inputs, trust rank and status.

  • Rank dictates how much you can currency you can hold before collecting it.
  • Status is how fast you can earn it.

Realm Companionship XP

The Serenita Teapot allows you to passively earn friendship XP for up to 8 characters that you own. The timer asks for 2 inputs, adeptal energy and trust rank.

  • Trust rank is how much can XP can be stored before collecting.
  • Adeptal energy is how fast you can earn the XP. (Adeptal energy a score of your teapot and how much is placed within it. It also determines your status.)

Fishing

Fishes respawn cooldown is usually 1 day once caught. Some have 3 day respawn times however. (Keep in mind that some fish appear only at day or night.)

Custom

The custom timer allows for people to make their own timers for whatever reason. There are three inputs: days, hours, minutes. You can put in over 24 hours or 60 minutes and it will still take it into account.
